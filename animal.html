Scottish Folds

The Scottish Fold is a medium-size cat with a rounded head and big round eyes, although he is known for his standout feature: ears that fold forward, giving him the appearance of a furry owl. His coat, which comes in many colors and patterns, can be short or long. The longhaired variety is known as the Highland Fold.

Deatils

A couple details about Scottish Folds:
  • male can weight from 9-13 pounds while females typically weight 6-9 pounds
  • The Scottish Fold comes in a number of colors and patterns, including solid, tabby, tabby and white, bicolor and particolor. Eye color depends on coat color. For instance, white and bicolor cats can have blue eyes or odd eyes (each eye is a different color).
  • they are breeded at home, so they are not found in the wild
  • celebrities such as Ed Sheeran and Taylor Swift have Scottish Folds
